My Notes from Chapter-04: Moving Average Process

  • Random Walk:
    • first difference is stationary with no auto-correlation

  • But what if stationary process exhibits auto-correlation. This can be modeled using Moving Average model, Autoregressive model or Auto-Regressive Moving Average model.
